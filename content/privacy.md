## Your Privacy Matters

At Christian Brothers Lining Co., we believe in respecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data when you visit our website.

## We Don't Use Cookies

This website does not use cookies. We don't track you across websites, we don't generate persistent identifiers, and we don't collect or store any personal or identifiable data.

## Privacy-Friendly Analytics

We use [Plausible Analytics](https://plausible.io/) to understand how visitors use our site. Plausible is a privacy-focused analytics tool that:

- **Does not use cookies** - No consent banners needed
- **Does not collect personal data** - No names, emails, or identifying information
- **Does not track you across sites** - Each visit is independent
- **Does not store IP addresses** - Your IP is never saved

### What We Can See

Our analytics show us aggregated, anonymous data only:

- Which pages are visited
- Where visitors come from (referral sources)
- General geographic region (country/city level)
- Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
- Browser and operating system

This information helps us understand what content is useful and how to improve our website. None of this data can be used to identify you personally.

## Data Storage

All analytics data is processed and stored in the European Union on servers in Germany, fully encrypted both in transit and at rest.

## GDPR & CCPA Compliance

Because we don't collect personal data, our website is compliant with:

- **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation)
- **CCPA** (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- **PECR** (Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations)

## Contact Form

If you contact us through our website, we will only use the information you provide (name, email, message) to respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to marketing lists or share your contact information with third parties.
