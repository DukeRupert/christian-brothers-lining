## DoD SkillBridge Program

We're proud to offer a **DoD SkillBridge opportunity** designed to prepare military members transitioning to the civilian sector with the expertise necessary to break into this innovative field.

Upon successful completion of the program, it is CBL's goal to offer **full-time employment** to motivated candidates.

*This opportunity is available ONLY to US Military members. Members can apply up to 1 year from separation/retirement and program participation begins within 180 days from their separation/retirement date.*

[Learn more about DoD SkillBridge →](https://skillbridge.osd.mil/index.htm)

### Eligibility
- Active Duty US Military member within 1 year of separation or retirement
- Must be 24 years of age or older
- Good driving record (for insurance purposes)

## Pipeline Rehabilitation Specialist

**Position:** Cured-In-Place-Pipe (CIPP) Installer
**Job Type:** Full-time
**Location:** In person - Fairfield, CA

Cured-In-Place Pipe (CIPP) is a trenchless pipe repair method where a resin-coated liner is inserted into an existing pipe, then hardened to form a seamless, durable pipe.

### Primary Responsibilities
- Install and cure CIPP liners for pipeline rehabilitation
- Prepare pipelines for inspection and installation using high-pressure water jetting
- Restore and reinforce existing manholes
- Conduct CCTV inspections of pipelines and installed CIPP for quality assurance
- Operate robotic equipment to reinstate lateral connections to main lines
- Potential to earn a Class A Commercial Driving License

### Compensation
**Pay:** $27.00 - $35.00 per hour ($56,160 - $72,800 annually) plus benefits
