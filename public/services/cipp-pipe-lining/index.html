CIPP Pipe Lining

Innovative trenchless technology that creates a new, corrosion-resistant pipe within your existing infrastructure. No excavation required.

Why Choose CIPP

Cost-Effective

Save significantly compared to traditional pipe replacement. No excavation means reduced labor and restoration costs.

Minimal Disruption

No digging required. Preserve your landscaping, roads, sidewalks, and structures during the rehabilitation process.

Long-Lasting

Creates a new, corrosion-resistant pipe within the old one. Our liners are designed to last 50+ years.

Quality Assured

Rigorous testing and inspection process ensures every installation meets our high standards.

Our CIPP Process

1

Inspection

Advanced CCTV inspection identifies pipe defects, assessing damage and determining CIPP suitability. We document the condition of your entire pipeline system.

2

Preparation & Cleaning

High-pressure water jetting removes debris, roots, and buildup. The pipe is thoroughly cleaned and prepared for liner installation.

3

Installation

A resin-saturated liner is inserted and positioned within the existing pipe, conforming precisely to its shape.

4

Curing & Reinstatement

The liner is cured in place using ambient, hot water, or UV light methods. Robotic equipment reinstates lateral connections to main lines.

5

Quality Control

Final CCTV inspection verifies proper installation, ensuring every project meets our rigorous quality standards.
